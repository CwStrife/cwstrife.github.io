## What to avoid
- Do not re-open old popup layout experiments unless a current bug proves they are needed.
- Do not treat missing store setup data as missing species data.
- Do not overload staff workflows with technical language.
- Do not add large visual effects everywhere; keep motion subtle and local.

## Preferred working style
- Make smaller, testable passes.
- Ship docs inside the zip.
- Keep one clear current-state summary and one compact version history.
- Favor stability and clarity over flashy experiments.
